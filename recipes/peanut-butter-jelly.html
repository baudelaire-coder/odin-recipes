Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A simple recipe for the classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons of grape jelly or 2 teaspoons strawberry jam

Steps

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  2. Spread the jelly onto the other side.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together to form a sandwich.
  4. Toddler adaptation: cut off crusts before serving.
