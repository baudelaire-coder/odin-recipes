Chopped Cheese Sandwich

Description

A chopped cheese is a New York classic consisting of chopped up burger patties and melted american cheese as the base, served on a kaiser roll or hero.

Ingredients

  • 4 hoagie buns
  • 1/4 cup mayonaise
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 1/4 pounds ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 8 slices American cheese
  • 1 cup lettuce
  • 1 large tomato

Steps

  1. Halve the buns and lightly toast them lightly. Spread one side with mayonnaise.
  2. In a non-stick pan, add the butter. Once melted, add the onion and cook until fragrant. Push to one side.
  3. Add the ground beef and do not break apart. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Sear for 4 minutes, flip, and cook for a further 2 minutes.
  4. Slowly break apart the beef then fold through the onions. Once there is no more pink in the center, divide the portion of beef into four equal sized portions.
  5. Add 2 slices of the American cheese over each portion and cook for 2 minutes, until gooey and melted.
  6. Transfer each portion into the hoagie buns, add shredded iceberg lettuce and sliced tomatoes.
